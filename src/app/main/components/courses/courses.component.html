Cyber Security Online Course

Description

This educational online course on the basics of information security is designed for college students, IT professionals, as well as owners of small and medium-sized businesses without information security departments. The course includes detailed information with analysis of real cases, online testing and practical exercises.

Course best suited for

College students, IT specialists, owners of small and medium businesses without information security departments.

Course Objectives

  • Learn the main areas of cybersecurity.
  • Prepare for certification in cybersecurity fundamentals.
  • Familiarize yourself with methods and publicly available tools for detecting cyber-attacks, protecting against them and restoring IT systems.

Duration

1 Month

Course Program

The Fundamentals of Cybersecurity course consists of four sections:
  • Network Security
  • Malware Analysis
  • Incident Response and Computer Forensics
  • Application Security

Course Result

Certificate

Fundamentals of Information Security

Description

Guided by National Research University Higher School of Economics,Dummy Academy has developed the Information Security module for the Digital Literacy online course, which will introduce students to a variety of cyber threats and provide basic information security knowledge. As part of the module, they analyse the basics of security in the 21st century: how not to lose access to a bank account and files on a personal PC.

Module Details -

The Fundamentals of Cybersecurity course consists of four sections:
  • Lesson 1. Types of threats: viruses, phishing, device vulnerability
  • Lesson 2. Virus protection: antiviruses, caution when downloading files
  • Lesson 3. Passwords: strength and precautions
  • Lesson 4. Banking data
  • Lesson 5. Spam and intrusive advertising

Course best suited for

Students and professionals whose field of activity is not related to IT

Cyber Security Certification

Description

The basic cybersecurity certification by ExtraSecureLabs Lab is an online test for beginners. It is intended to assess the general level of knowledge in the field of information security and identify areas in which additional training is needed.

Course best suited for

Anyone who wants to test their level of knowledge of the basics of cybersecurity

What is it for?

  • Assess the general level of knowledge in the field of cybersecurity.
  • Identify areas where additional training is needed.

Certification and Training

This test includes 9 sections:
  • Basic concepts of cybersecurity.
  • Computer architecture.
  • Operating Systems
  • Malware analysis
  • Corporate cybersecurity
  • Cryptography
  • Network Security
  • Application Protection
  • Incident response and computer forensic
